The company Hotline has been defunct for 2 decades. But the passion of the community is alive and well.

Hotline has always been a place of sharing, a freedom of hosting your own server, allowing people to discover your creations, your content, your passion.

Thanks to the team from Hotline Wiki and Stickytack, we've been able to preserve the domains associated with Hotline such as hlserver.com (your first stop on Hotline), hltracker.com (your portal to other servers and awesome things, and now bigredh.com (the home to the old Hotline Empire).

At one point over 3 million people worldwide used this software. Its widdled down to having about 6 active servers, but with some new development has grown greatly in 2024.

So welcome friends, bring your ideas, bring your downloads, bring your fun. And join us.

Welcome to Hotline. - Ari and Tagban (LostArch)
